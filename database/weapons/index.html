Mina the Hollower Weapons Guide

Mina has five main weapons, each built around a different combat rhythm. This page compares their reach, speed, pressure, defensive value, and ideal playstyle so you can choose the right weapon before building a complete setup.

For full equipment combinations, use the Build Planner or read the Best Builds Guide.

How to read the bars: These are relative editorial comparisons, not official in-game damage, range, or frame data. They show how each weapon feels compared with the other available weapons.

Nightstar offers excellent reach and safe spacing. Its balanced rhythm makes it a reliable weapon for learning enemy patterns and controlling groups without standing directly beside them.

Strengths: Long reach, safe positioning, and dependable crowd control.

Weaknesses: Lower burst pressure than more specialized close-range weapons.

Best for: beginners, exploration, and learning boss patterns

Whisper & Vesper reward close-range pressure and quick reactions. They feel strongest when you can stay near the target and keep attacking through safe openings.

Strengths: Fast attacks, strong pressure, and responsive movement.

Weaknesses: Short reach leaves less room for positioning mistakes.

Best for: aggressive players and high-pressure boss attempts

Blaststrike Maul is built around deliberate, high-impact attacks. It works best when you wait for clear openings instead of pressing buttons continuously.

Weaknesses: Slow attacks can be punished when used carelessly.

Mina the Hollower Weapons FAQ

What is the best weapon for beginners?

Nightstar is a strong starting option because its reach allows safer spacing while learning enemy and boss patterns.

Which weapon is best for aggressive players?

Whisper & Vesper favor close-range pressure and rapid attacks, but the short reach demands confident positioning.

Which weapon is best for defensive play?

Guardian Casket is the natural choice for players who prefer defense, patience, and counterattacks.

Can every weapon complete the game?

Yes. Weapon choice is mainly about comfort, encounter type, and how the rest of the build supports the weapon.

Where can I create a full build?

Use the Build Planner to combine a weapon, Sidearm, and up to six Trinkets.
