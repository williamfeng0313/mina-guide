Nether Kraken

Shipwreck • Optional

Encounter Overview

Mina first encounters the Nether Kraken during the scripted prologue sequence on the ship deck. That opening encounter is survived rather than won. After the shipwreck, returning to the upper deck can trigger the optional full encounter.

Key Strategy

Watch the Kraken's clawed arms. Each arm pulls back before slamming the deck, giving you time to move away from the target area. Once the claw lands, step in for a short attack window, then retreat before the next slam.

Avoid long attack strings. The safest rhythm is to dodge the slam, punish the exposed arm briefly, and reset your position.

Reward

Defeating the optional Nether Kraken encounter awards the Uranium Bracelet .
