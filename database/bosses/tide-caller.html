Tide Caller

Nox's Bayou • Normal

Key Strategy

Keep the fight on solid ground and avoid stepping into the water while trying to maintain pressure. A ranged weapon such as Battery Buster can help you attack without giving up safe positioning.

Wait for clear attack windows instead of chasing Tide Caller across the arena. Reposition first, then deal damage from a stable platform.

Common Mistake

Do not sacrifice your footing just to continue a combo. Falling into the water or being forced into a poor angle usually costs more damage than a missed attack window.
