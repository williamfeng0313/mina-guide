Major Miner

Sifted Sands • Normal

Key Strategy

During phase two, watch for the TNT carts and use the arena hazards against Major Miner instead of forcing long attack strings. A ranged option such as Battery Buster can help maintain pressure while giving you more room to react.

Prioritize safe positioning, wait for clear recovery windows, and avoid standing in the path of the carts while trying to deal damage.
