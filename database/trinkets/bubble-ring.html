Bubble Ring

ossex

Quick Answer: Bubble Ring

Bubble Ring is a Defense Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It lets Mina form a protective shield after striking foes quickly, helping absorb one hit during combat, exploration, and dangerous cleanup routes.

If you are collecting every Trinket or trying to finish 100% completion, use this page together with the All Trinket Locations, Missable Trinkets, and 100% Cleanup Checklist.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Strike foes quickly to form a protective shield that absorbs one hit.
  • Location: Ossex — Purchased from Lady Susan after the Trinket Bazaar expands.
  • Category: Defense.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 750 Bones.

Is Bubble Ring Worth Using?

Bubble Ring is worth using if you want more forgiveness while learning enemy patterns, exploring dangerous rooms, or revisiting areas during cleanup. It is not always the fastest offensive option, but it can make risky routes feel more consistent.

The shield absorbs one hit. Landing another successful chain of quick attacks refreshes it.

For 100% cleanup, Bubble Ring works best as a safety Trinket. Use it when your goal is to avoid mistakes, protect a run, or safely check old rooms for missed items.

Best Uses for Bubble Ring

  • Safer boss learning when you are still reading patterns.
  • Exploration routes where avoiding chip damage matters.
  • Survival builds for cautious players.
  • Safer backtracking through enemy-heavy rooms during late-game cleanup.
