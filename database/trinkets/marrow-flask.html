Marrow Flask

Backwaters

Effect

Marrow Flask increases the value of Bone pickups by 25%, making it a useful utility Trinket for players who want to collect Bones faster.

Location

Backwaters — found inside a standard chest in the eastern section of the area.

Best Use

Equip Marrow Flask while exploring enemy-dense areas or farming Bones for upgrades, purchases, and other progression costs. It offers no direct combat bonus, so replace it with a defensive or offensive Trinket before a difficult boss fight.

Is Marrow Flask Worth Using?

Marrow Flask is worthwhile when Bone income is your priority. Its value falls during boss fights and other encounters where survivability or damage matters more than currency gain.
