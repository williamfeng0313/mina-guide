Flame Guard

wilds

Quick Answer: Flame Guard

Flame Guard is a Defense Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It blocks one hit from fire or explosions, then breaks and recharges after a few seconds. It is especially useful in areas and encounters with repeated fire or explosive hazards.

If you are collecting every Trinket or trying to finish 100% completion, use this page together with the All Trinket Locations, Missable Trinkets, and 100% Cleanup Checklist.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Blocks one hit from fire or explosions, then breaks. Recharges after a few seconds.
  • Location: Western Wilds — Molten Foundry.
  • Category: Defense.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 750 Bones.

Is Flame Guard Worth Using?

Flame Guard is worth using in areas or encounters where fire and explosive damage are common. Because it blocks one qualifying hit and then recharges after a few seconds, it can repeatedly prevent avoidable damage during longer exploration routes.

It is much less valuable against enemies or bosses that do not use fire or explosions, so it is best treated as a situational Defense Trinket rather than a permanent part of every build.

Best Uses for Flame Guard

  • Exploring areas with frequent fire hazards.
  • Encounters with explosive attacks or environmental explosions.
  • Safer traversal through the Molten Foundry and similar hazard-heavy rooms.
  • Defensive builds that value preventing one dangerous hit.
