Oozing Organ

kindlewood

Quick Answer: Oozing Organ

Oozing Organ is an Attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It leaves a damaging trail of ooze behind Mina whenever she burrows, turning her movement path into a temporary hazard for enemies.

Oozing Organ is obtained in Kindlewood. Buy it from the vendor inside the Madd House, located just outside the farm area.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Leave a damaging trail of ooze behind you whenever you burrow.
  • Location: Kindlewood — Madd House.
  • How to Get: Buy Oozing Organ from the vendor inside the Madd House, just outside the farm area in Kindlewood.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 1,000 Bones.

How to Get Oozing Organ

  1. Travel to Kindlewood.
  2. Head toward the farm area.
  3. Find the Madd House located just outside the farm.
  4. Enter the Madd House and speak to the vendor inside.
  5. Purchase Oozing Organ from the vendor.

Oozing Organ is a shop purchase rather than a chest reward, so check the Madd House vendor if it is missing from your collection.

How Does Oozing Organ Work?

Oozing Organ creates a damaging ooze trail along Mina's burrow path. Enemies that cross or remain inside the trail can take damage.

The Trinket is most effective when Mina burrows through crowded rooms or positions the trail where enemies are likely to move.

Is Oozing Organ Worth Using?

Oozing Organ is worth using if burrowing is already a major part of your combat style. It adds damage to movement that Mina would often perform anyway.

It works best against groups, slow enemies, or enemies that repeatedly cross the same space. The ooze trail can turn narrow paths and crowded rooms into damaging zones.

It is less valuable if you rarely burrow during combat or if enemies move away from the trail before taking meaningful damage.

Best Uses for Oozing Organ

  • Burrow-heavy attack builds.
  • Damaging groups of enemies while repositioning.
  • Creating hazardous paths in narrow rooms.
  • Adding passive damage during defensive burrow movement.
  • Fighting slow enemies that remain near the ooze trail.

Oozing Organ and Burrow Builds

Oozing Organ fits naturally into builds that already reward frequent burrowing. It turns traversal and repositioning into another source of damage.

Pair it with other burrow-focused Trinkets when you want Mina's entry, movement path, and emergence to contribute to combat.

Oozing Organ and 100% Cleanup

Oozing Organ counts toward the complete Trinket collection. Because it is purchased from a vendor rather than found in a chest, it can be overlooked during final cleanup.

If it is missing from your collection, return to the Madd House in Kindlewood and check the vendor inside. You can also compare your save against the All Trinket Locations guide or use the Checklist and Tracker Tool .
