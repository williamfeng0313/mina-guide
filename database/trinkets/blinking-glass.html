Blinking Glass

coltrane peak

Quick Answer: Blinking Glass

Blinking Glass is a Defense Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It sometimes allows Mina to avoid taking damage and become briefly invulnerable.

Blinking Glass is found in Coltrane Peak. Its value comes from occasionally preventing a hit entirely and giving Mina a short window of invulnerability.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Sometimes, avoid taking damage, and become briefly invulnerable.
  • Location: Coltrane Peak.
  • Category: Defense.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.

How Does Blinking Glass Work?

Blinking Glass gives Mina a chance to avoid incoming damage. When the effect triggers, Mina also becomes briefly invulnerable.

Because the effect is not guaranteed on every hit, it should be treated as extra protection rather than something you can rely on for precise timing.

Is Blinking Glass Worth Using?

Blinking Glass is useful for players who want an additional layer of passive protection. The chance to avoid damage can occasionally save a run or reduce mistakes during difficult fights.

Its main weakness is consistency. Because the effect only triggers sometimes, it is less dependable than defensive Trinkets with guaranteed activation conditions.

Best Uses for Blinking Glass

  • Defensive builds that value passive damage prevention.
  • Long exploration routes where avoiding one mistake can matter.
  • Boss learning where occasional damage prevention adds extra safety.
  • Players who prefer survivability over maximum damage output.

Blinking Glass and 100% Completion

Blinking Glass counts toward the complete Trinket collection. If it is missing from your save, check Coltrane Peak during your final Trinket cleanup.
