Polyp Lamp

Radiant Manor

Effect

Polyp Lamp illuminates dark areas and can stun enemies caught in its light, giving Mina more control in poorly lit rooms and crowded encounters.

Location

Radiant Manor — found inside a standard chest in the basement.

Best Use

Polyp Lamp is most useful while exploring dark rooms or dealing with groups of enemies that can be interrupted by the light effect. It is less valuable in bright areas or encounters where enemies cannot be kept inside the illuminated zone.

Is Polyp Lamp Worth Using?

It is a situational exploration Trinket rather than a universal combat choice. Equip it when visibility and crowd control are the main problems, then switch to a stronger offensive or defensive option for encounters where the light effect adds little value.
