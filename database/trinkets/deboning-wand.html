Deboning Wand

ossex

Quick Answer: Deboning Wand

Deboning Wand is a Utility Trinket in Mina the Hollower. Enemies reward significantly more Bones, but the dropped Bones bounce around, making them harder to collect.

Deboning Wand is obtained in Ossex as a reward from Elizabeth.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Enemies reward far more Bones, but the Bones bounce around.
  • Location: Ossex — Reward from Elizabeth.
  • Category: Utility.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 500 Bones.

How Does Deboning Wand Work?

Deboning Wand increases the amount of Bones earned from defeated enemies, but the extra currency scatters and bounces instead of dropping neatly in place.

This makes it an economy-focused Trinket: you earn more Bones overall, but you may need to spend extra time chasing and collecting the drops.

Is Deboning Wand Worth Using?

Deboning Wand is useful when you want to farm Bones for shops, upgrades, or other purchases. The extra currency can add up quickly during enemy-heavy routes.

Its drawback is convenience. Because the Bones bounce around, you may lose some drops or spend more time collecting them than with a normal setup.

Best Uses for Deboning Wand

  • Farming Bones for expensive purchases.
  • Enemy-heavy routes with many repeatable encounters.
  • Economy-focused builds.
  • Players willing to trade convenience for increased Bone income.

Deboning Wand and 100% Completion

Deboning Wand is tied to Elizabeth in Ossex rather than a normal chest or shop purchase. If it is missing from your collection, check whether you have completed the relevant interaction with Elizabeth.
