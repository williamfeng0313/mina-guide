Bellows Bustle

utility

Quick Answer: Bellows Bustle

Bellows Bustle is a Utility Trinket in Mina the Hollower. Press Jump while airborne to perform an air dash, giving Mina extra horizontal movement for gap-crossing, repositioning, and exploration.

To get Bellows Bustle, complete all three races hosted by Blaise in Sandfalls, Ossex, and the Western Wilds.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Press Jump while airborne to perform an air dash.
  • How to Get: Complete all three races hosted by Blaise.
  • Race Locations: Sandfalls, Ossex, and Western Wilds.
  • Category: Utility.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 1,000 Bones.

How to Get Bellows Bustle

  1. Find Blaise and complete his race in Sandfalls.
  2. Complete Blaise's race in Ossex.
  3. Complete Blaise's race in the Western Wilds.
  4. After completing all three races, receive Bellows Bustle as the reward.

How Does Bellows Bustle Work?

Bellows Bustle adds an extra mid-air movement option. After jumping, press Jump again while airborne to dash forward.

The air dash can help Mina cross wider gaps, correct a jump, or quickly reposition during combat.

Is Bellows Bustle Worth Using?

Bellows Bustle is one of the more useful mobility Trinkets if you value movement and exploration. The air dash can make difficult jumps more forgiving and helps Mina reposition quickly during combat.

It is less useful in situations where movement is not the main problem, but for exploration-heavy routes and difficult platforming sections, the extra air control can be extremely useful.

Best Uses for Bellows Bustle

  • Crossing wider gaps.
  • Correcting jumps while airborne.
  • Repositioning quickly during combat.
  • Exploration routes with difficult platforming.
  • Late-game cleanup where mobility helps reduce backtracking mistakes.

Bellows Bustle and 100% Completion

Bellows Bustle is tied to Blaise's race circuit rather than a standard chest or shop, so it can be overlooked if you have not completed every race.

If Bellows Bustle is missing from your collection, check whether you have completed Blaise's races in Sandfalls, Ossex, and the Western Wilds.
