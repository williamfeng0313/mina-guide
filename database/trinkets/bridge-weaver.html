Bridge Weaver

astral orrery

Quick Answer: Bridge Weaver

Bridge Weaver is a Utility Trinket in Mina the Hollower. Step off a ledge to summon a web-weaving spider that helps Mina cross gaps.

Bridge Weaver is found in a chest in the Astral Orrery.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Step off a ledge to summon a web-weaving spider and cross gaps.
  • Location: Astral Orrery — Found in a chest.
  • Category: Utility.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 1,250 Bones.

How Does Bridge Weaver Work?

When Mina steps off a ledge, Bridge Weaver summons a spider that creates a temporary web footing, allowing her to continue moving across gaps.

This makes Bridge Weaver primarily an exploration and traversal Trinket rather than a direct combat upgrade.

Is Bridge Weaver Worth Using?

Bridge Weaver is useful when exploring areas with difficult gaps or when revisiting locations during collectible cleanup.

It does not directly increase Mina's damage or survivability, so its value depends heavily on the area you are exploring.

Best Uses for Bridge Weaver

  • Crossing difficult gaps during exploration.
  • Reaching areas that are awkward to traverse normally.
  • Collectible cleanup and late-game backtracking.
  • Mobility-focused exploration builds.

Bridge Weaver and 100% Completion

Bridge Weaver is found in the Astral Orrery, so check that area carefully if the Trinket is missing from your collection.
