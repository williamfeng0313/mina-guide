Watchful Eye

other

Quick Answer: Watchful Eye

Watchful Eye is a utility Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It rewards clean play by granting extra Bones when you defeat a foe without taking damage, making it useful for careful exploration, farming, and upgrade planning.

If you are collecting every Trinket, farming Bones for upgrades, or checking your final completion route, use Watchful Eye together with the All Trinket Locations, Missable Trinkets, and 100% Cleanup Checklist.

Effect: Earn extra Bones for defeating a foe unharmed, as judged worthy by the eye.

📍 Location: Coltrane Peak — Purchased from Rupert.

Is Watchful Eye Worth Using?

Watchful Eye is worth using if you can defeat enemies without taking damage and want extra Bones over time. It is most useful for careful players, farming routes, upgrade planning, and safer exploration where you are already avoiding hits.

It is less useful during messy boss attempts or difficult rooms where you expect to take damage. For 100% cleanup, Watchful Eye is best treated as an economy Trinket rather than a combat or secret-detection Trinket.

Best Uses for Watchful Eye

  • Farming extra Bones from enemies you can defeat safely.
  • Building up resources for upgrades, purchases, or route preparation.
  • Careful exploration where you can avoid taking damage consistently.
  • Early or mid-game economy planning before expensive purchases.
  • Completion cleanup when you need extra Bones for remaining upgrades or shop items.

Watchful Eye and 100% Cleanup

Watchful Eye helps 100% cleanup indirectly by giving careful players more Bones to work with. It does not reveal hidden rooms, but it can support upgrade routes, shop purchases, and late-game preparation.

If you are checking missing Trinkets or secret routes, pair this page with the All Trinket Locations guide and the Checklist & Tracker Tool instead of relying on Watchful Eye as a detection item.
