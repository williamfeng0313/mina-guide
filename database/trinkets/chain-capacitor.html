Chain Capacitor

eastern heath

Quick Answer: Chain Capacitor

Chain Capacitor is an Attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. Landing consecutive attacks without missing increases your damage, while a missed attack resets the combo.

To get Chain Capacitor, complete Wrecker's Choppe Shoppe challenge in Eastern Heath by destroying the vehicle within 30 seconds.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Attack enemies without missing to boost damage. Missing resets the combo.
  • Location: Eastern Heath — Choppe Shoppe.
  • How to Get: Complete Wrecker's vehicle-destruction challenge within 30 seconds.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 750 Bones.

How Does Chain Capacitor Work?

Chain Capacitor rewards accuracy. Consecutive successful attacks build bonus damage, but swinging and missing resets the combo.

The Trinket works best when you can maintain a steady attack rhythm and avoid unnecessary attacks that might break the chain.

How to Get Chain Capacitor

  1. Travel to the Choppe Shoppe in Eastern Heath.
  2. Start Wrecker's vehicle-destruction challenge.
  3. Destroy the vehicle within 30 seconds.
  4. Complete the challenge to receive Chain Capacitor.

Is Chain Capacitor Worth Using?

Chain Capacitor can be very strong for accurate players who consistently land attacks. The longer you maintain the combo, the more value you get from the Trinket.

It is less forgiving in chaotic fights where enemies move unpredictably or where missed attacks are common, because one whiff resets the combo.

Best Uses for Chain Capacitor

  • Boss fights where you can maintain consistent attack windows.
  • Fast or precise weapons that make consecutive hits easier.
  • Damage-focused builds that reward clean execution.
  • Players who are comfortable avoiding unnecessary attacks.

Chain Capacitor and Combo Conductor

Chain Capacitor is also tied to the Combo Conductor achievement, which requires maintaining the maximum Chain Capacitor combo during combat for 10 seconds.
