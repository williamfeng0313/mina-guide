Lace Glove

southern outskirts / western wilds

Quick Answer: Lace Glove

Lace Glove is a Trinket in Mina the Hollower that raises Mina's effective Attack level by one.

Lace Glove is tied to the Ack and Dak encounter sequence, which spans the Southern Outskirts and Western Wilds.

Lace Glove Details

  • Effect: Raises Attack level by one.
  • Location: Southern Outskirts / Western Wilds.
  • How to Get: Progress the Ack and Dak encounter sequence involving Bugin.

How to Get Lace Glove

  1. Find Ack and Dak during their encounter sequence in the Southern Outskirts and Western Wilds.
  2. Continue the associated Bugin encounters.
  3. Progress the sequence until Lace Glove is awarded.

The Lace Glove is associated with more than one encounter location, so it is better treated as a short NPC encounter chain rather than a single chest or shop pickup.

How Does Lace Glove Work?

Lace Glove raises Mina's effective Attack level by one while equipped.

Unlike conditional damage Trinkets, Lace Glove provides a straightforward offensive boost without requiring low health, a combo, or a specific attack pattern.

Is Lace Glove Worth Using?

Lace Glove is a simple offensive option for players who want a reliable Attack increase without managing a special trigger condition.

Its value depends on whether you prefer a flat Attack boost or a more specialized Trinket that provides stronger benefits under specific conditions.

Best Uses for Lace Glove

  • General-purpose offensive builds.
  • Players who want a simple, always-active Attack boost.
  • Boss fights where consistent damage matters.
  • Early and mid-game progression before more specialized builds are available.
