Burning Beastium

coltrane peak

Quick Answer: Burning Beastium

Burning Beastium is an Attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. When Mina takes a hit, it creates damaging fireballs around her. You must jump or burrow to avoid the fireballs yourself.

Burning Beastium is found in Coltrane Peak.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Create damaging fireballs when hit. Jump or burrow to avoid them.
  • Location: Coltrane Peak.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 1,250 Bones.

How Does Burning Beastium Work?

Burning Beastium is a reactive Trinket. Its effect triggers when Mina takes damage, causing dangerous fireballs to appear around her.

The fireballs can damage enemies, but Mina also needs to avoid them by jumping or burrowing. This makes the Trinket powerful but risky compared with more straightforward damage upgrades.

Is Burning Beastium Worth Using?

Burning Beastium can be useful if you are comfortable managing its risk. Taking a hit can create extra damage opportunities, especially when enemies remain close to Mina.

It is less suitable for players who prefer predictable defensive setups, because its effect creates an additional hazard that Mina must immediately avoid.

Best Uses for Burning Beastium

  • Aggressive builds that can capitalize on reactive damage.
  • Fights where enemies stay close after hitting Mina.
  • Players comfortable using jumps and burrows to avoid the spawned fireballs.
  • Beastium-focused setups.

Burning Beastium and 100% Completion

Burning Beastium is one of the Beastium-family Trinkets and counts toward the complete Trinket collection. If it is missing from your save, check Coltrane Peak during your final cleanup.
