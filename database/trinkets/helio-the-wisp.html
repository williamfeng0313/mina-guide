Helio the Wisp

western wilds

Quick Answer: Helio the Wisp

Helio the Wisp is a healing-focused Trinket in Mina the Hollower. After Mina takes damage, it summons a friendly wisp that restores health.

You can buy Helio the Wisp from Poppit in Western Wilds for 400 Bones.

Helio the Wisp Details

  • Effect: After taking damage, summon a friendly wisp that restores your health.
  • Location: Western Wilds.
  • How to Get: Purchase Helio the Wisp from Poppit.
  • Purchase Price: 400 Bones.

How to Get Helio the Wisp

  1. Travel to Western Wilds.
  2. Find Poppit's shop in the area.
  3. Open the shop inventory and select Helio the Wisp.
  4. Purchase it for 400 Bones.

How Does Helio the Wisp Work?

Helio the Wisp activates after Mina takes damage. A friendly wisp appears and restores health, giving the Trinket a defensive recovery role rather than a direct damage bonus.

This makes Helio useful when learning difficult encounters, especially when repeated mistakes are slowly draining your health.

Is Helio the Wisp Worth Using?

Helio the Wisp is a useful survival option for players who value recovery after taking damage. It can be especially helpful while learning enemy patterns or progressing through difficult areas.

Its value is lower for players who rarely get hit or prefer pure damage, movement, or resource-focused Trinkets.

Best Uses for Helio the Wisp

  • Learning difficult boss patterns.
  • Exploration routes where healing resources are limited.
  • Survival-focused builds.
  • Players who prefer recovery over maximum damage.
