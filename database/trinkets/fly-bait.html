Fly Bait

queensbury crypt

Quick Answer: Fly Bait

Fly Bait is an Attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. Defeating enemies attracts friendly flies that attack other foes, giving Mina passive extra damage during longer combat encounters.

Fly Bait is obtained in Queensbury Crypt as a reward from Midden.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Defeat enemies to attract friendly flies that attack other foes.
  • Location: Queensbury Crypt.
  • How to Get: Receive Fly Bait as a reward from Midden.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 500 Bones.

How Does Fly Bait Work?

Fly Bait creates friendly flies when Mina defeats enemies. The flies orbit Mina and attack other foes automatically, turning enemy kills into extra passive damage.

Mina can have up to four flies active at once, making the Trinket more useful in rooms with multiple enemies than in encounters with only one target.

How to Get Fly Bait

  1. Progress through Queensbury Crypt.
  2. Reach the Ancestral Chamber and find Midden.
  3. Complete Midden's encounter and continue his sequence.
  4. Receive Fly Bait as Midden's reward.

Is Fly Bait Worth Using?

Fly Bait is most useful in areas with several enemies, where one kill can start building a group of friendly flies that help clear the rest of the room.

It is less effective in single-target encounters where there are no weaker enemies available to trigger the summon effect.

Best Uses for Fly Bait

  • Rooms with multiple enemies.
  • Exploration routes with frequent combat encounters.
  • Passive damage builds.
  • Players who want extra damage without additional inputs.
  • Encounters where weaker enemies can be defeated first to build up flies.
