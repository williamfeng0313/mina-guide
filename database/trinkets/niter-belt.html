Niter Belt

sandfalls

Quick Answer: Niter Belt

Niter Belt is an attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It creates a radial explosion when Mina emerges from burrow, making it useful for aggressive burrow-based combat and clearing enemies around your exit point.

Niter Belt is found in Sandfalls. It is best for players who often use burrow offensively instead of treating burrow only as movement or evasion.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Creates a radial explosion when emerging from burrow.
  • Location: Sandfalls.
  • How to Get: Check Sandfalls during your Trinket cleanup route.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.

Is Niter Belt Worth Using?

Niter Belt is worth using if your playstyle already uses burrow to approach enemies, reposition, or pop out near targets. The radial explosion can turn burrow exits into offensive opportunities.

It is less useful if you mainly use burrow defensively or avoid emerging near enemies. In that case, safer utility or defense Trinkets may feel more consistent.

Best Uses for Niter Belt

  • Burrow-heavy combat routes.
  • Rooms where enemies cluster near Mina's exit point.
  • Aggressive playstyles that use burrow as an attack setup.
  • Clearing small enemies while repositioning.
  • Players who want more damage from movement decisions.

Niter Belt and 100% Cleanup

Niter Belt is mainly a combat Trinket, but it can still help during cleanup if you are revisiting enemy-heavy rooms. It rewards players who are already comfortable using burrow as part of their route.

If Niter Belt is missing from your collection, check Sandfalls and compare your save against the All Trinket Locations checklist.
