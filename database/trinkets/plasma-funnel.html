Plasma Funnel

ossex

Quick Answer: Plasma Funnel

Plasma Funnel is a Defense Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It lets Mina drink Plasma Vials twice as fast and prevents stored Vials from being lost when she is hit.

Plasma Funnel can be purchased from Lady Susan at the Trinket Bazaar in Ossex.

Plasma Funnel Details

  • Effect: Drink Plasma Vials twice as fast. Don't lose Plasma Vials when hit.
  • Location: Ossex — Trinket Bazaar.
  • How to Get: Purchase Plasma Funnel from Lady Susan.
  • Category: Defense.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 350 Bones.

How to Get Plasma Funnel

  1. Reach Ossex.
  2. Visit Lady Susan's Trinket Bazaar.
  3. Check the available Trinket stock.
  4. Purchase Plasma Funnel when it is available.

How Does Plasma Funnel Work?

Plasma Funnel improves both healing speed and Vial security. Its first effect halves the time needed to drink a Plasma Vial, making healing less risky during fast fights.

Its second effect prevents Mina from losing stored Plasma Vials when hit, helping preserve healing resources during difficult encounters.

Is Plasma Funnel Worth Using?

Plasma Funnel is a strong defensive comfort Trinket for players who rely heavily on Plasma Vials and want safer healing during combat.

It is especially useful in boss fights or difficult areas where long healing animations can leave Mina vulnerable.

Best Uses for Plasma Funnel

  • Boss fights where healing windows are short.
  • Defensive and survival-focused builds.
  • Players who frequently rely on Plasma Vials.
  • Long exploration or cleanup routes where preserving healing resources matters.
