Spring Heels

radiant-manor

Quick Answer: Spring Heels

Spring Heels is a movement-focused Trinket in Mina the Hollower. It lets Mina jump after landing to perform a longer, higher jump, making it useful for traversal, spacing, and cleanup routes.

Spring Heels is found in Radiant Manor. It is best used by players who want better movement control while exploring, backtracking, or reaching awkward areas during 100% completion.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: Jump after landing to perform a longer, higher jump.
  • Location: Radiant Manor.
  • How to Get: Check Radiant Manor during your Trinket cleanup route.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.

Is Spring Heels Worth Using?

Spring Heels is worth using if you like movement Trinkets or want smoother traversal during exploration. The longer, higher jump can help with spacing, platforming, and reaching areas more comfortably.

It is less useful if you are looking for direct damage or passive survival. For boss learning, defensive Trinkets like Bell of Grace, Bubble Ring, or Dodging Pendulum may feel more reliable.

Best Uses for Spring Heels

  • Traversal and movement-heavy exploration.
  • Backtracking through areas with awkward jumps or enemy spacing.
  • Players who want a more mobile cleanup route.
  • Rooms where better jumping helps avoid contact damage.
  • 100% completion routes where movement comfort matters.

Spring Heels and 100% Cleanup

Spring Heels can make cleanup routes more comfortable by improving Mina's movement after landing. It is especially useful when revisiting areas, checking missed rooms, or moving through platforming-heavy sections.

If Spring Heels is missing from your collection, check Radiant Manor and compare your save against the All Trinket Locations checklist.
