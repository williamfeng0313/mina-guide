Reckless Beastium

mirror's end

Quick Answer: Reckless Beastium

Reckless Beastium is an Attack Trinket in Mina the Hollower. After Mina heals, her next few hits deal increased damage, but she also becomes more vulnerable.

Reckless Beastium is obtained in Mirror's End as a reward from Dr. Naught. It is designed for aggressive players who can capitalize on the temporary damage window without taking unnecessary hits.

Trinket Details

  • Effect: After healing, your next few hits are powered up, but you're more vulnerable.
  • Location: Mirror's End — Reward from Dr. Naught.
  • How to Get: Progress through Mirror's End and complete the required interaction or encounter involving Dr. Naught to receive Reckless Beastium.
  • Category: Attack.
  • Slot Cost: 1 Trinket slot.
  • Sell Value: 1,000 Bones.

How Does Reckless Beastium Work?

Healing activates a temporary offensive window. Mina's next few successful attacks are powered up, allowing her to turn a defensive action into immediate counter-pressure.

The tradeoff is increased vulnerability. Reckless Beastium rewards players who heal at safe moments and immediately use the damage boost without exposing themselves to avoidable attacks.

Is Reckless Beastium Worth Using?

Reckless Beastium is worth using if you prefer aggressive builds and already understand enemy attack patterns. The post-heal damage boost can create strong burst opportunities during boss fights and difficult combat rooms.

It is less reliable for cautious players because its defensive downside can punish poor positioning. Healing at the wrong time may leave Mina exposed before she can use the powered-up attacks.

Treat the Trinket as a risk-reward damage tool, not a general survival option.

Best Uses for Reckless Beastium

  • Boss fights with clear healing windows.
  • Burst-damage builds.
  • Aggressive playstyles that attack immediately after healing.
  • Players who already know enemy patterns.
  • Short damage phases where a few powered-up hits matter.

Reckless Beastium Build Tips

  • Heal only when you have enough space to attack safely afterward.
  • Use the powered-up hits quickly instead of waiting for a perfect opening.
  • Avoid pairing the Trinket with an overly defensive playstyle.
  • Learn boss recovery windows before relying on the damage boost.
  • Do not assume healing makes Mina safe while the vulnerability penalty is active.

Reckless Beastium and 100% Cleanup

Reckless Beastium counts toward the complete Trinket collection. Because it is tied to Dr. Naught rather than an obvious standard chest, it can be overlooked during final cleanup.

If it is missing from your collection, revisit Mirror's End and check your progress with Dr. Naught. You can also compare your save against the All Trinket Locations guide or use the Checklist and Tracker Tool.
