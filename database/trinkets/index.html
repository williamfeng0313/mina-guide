Bubble Ring
Strike foes quickly to form a protective shield that absorbs one hit.

Counter Vial
Dodge and launch a burst at the attacker if struck while readying a vial.
📍 Ossex — Trinket Bazaar shop
Deboning Wand
Enemies reward far more Bones, but the Bones bounce around erratically.
📍 Ossex — Reward from Elizabeth at Ossex manor. Accept her offer, then repay 1,000 Bones.
Steady Soles
Move normally on water, grass, stairs, and ice. Resist knockback, even into pits.
📍 Ossex — Enter the house right of the fountain via the roof. Burrow under barricade, go through attic.
Valor Medallion
Move slightly while healing. No Plasma lost when struck during a heal.
📍 Ossex — Speak to Furl above the clothes store, hit his nose, bring him flowers, then burrow-boop him.
Bell of Grace
Chance to keep Spark on death. On sparkless death, save half or all Bones.
📍 Ossex — Attack Frayd to remove his Bones, then give him 1,000 Bones at the homeless camp.
Pneumatic Armlet
Supercharge your throws. Thrown objects go faster, further, and deal more damage.
📍 Ossex — Reward from Willis in the Ossex Music Hall.
Warding Beastium
Take less damage, but healing is much less effective.
📍 Ossex — Near Evra in the city.
Thermal Pack
Boosts damage after death or exiting Underlab. Effect fades, then returns stronger.
📍 Ossex — Return to the Miner's Den after saving Fiancé from Bone Beach.
Keri the Wisp
Press Jump while airborne to float and extend your fall distance.
📍 Southern Outskirts — Poppit the Merchant's secret cave near the trail to Bone Beach.
Primed Vial Pouch
Carry 2 extra Plasma Vials. Using Vials always restores some health.
📍 Southern Outskirts — Small house entered via the roof; chest across a few gaps inside.
Smelling Salts
Raises Sidearm level by one.
📍 Eastern Heath — Frozen Pass, accessed via the fast travel train system.
Willow the Wisp
After taking damage, summon a friendly wisp that boosts attack damage and speed.
📍 Eastern Heath — Poppit the Merchant's secret cave.
Windfall Charm
Picking up Joules restores some Plasma. Foes hit by Sidearms can drop Joules.
📍 Eastern Heath — Obtained from Lad, the kid flying the green kite.
Chain Capacitor
Attack enemies without missing to boost damage. Missing resets the combo.
📍 Eastern Heath — Destroy the Choppe Shoppe's vehicle within the time limit.
Twill Weave
Raises Defense level by one.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Cliff reached by rope near the big watery section at the start.
Vascular Syrup
Health decreases very slowly after being hit. You cannot die until the effect runs out.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Near Big Lagoon.
Pit Preserver
Bounce up when falling into a pit or water. Pit damage is reduced.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Buy from the Kindly Hag merchant in her house, 1,000 Bones.
Iron Lung
Increases maximum burrow time, even when underwater.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Reward for defeating the Mock Moon mini boss.
Tumbling Tutu
Press Jump while airborne to spin attack and bounce off enemies.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Return the small frog to the Bayou Band in Backwaters after beating Nox's Beast.
Volatile Beastium
Defeated enemies sometimes drop a bomb.
📍 Nox's Bayou — Buy from a merchant encountered on the Bone Beach–to–Bayou train route.
Helio the Wisp

Flame Guard

Dead Leaf

Lace Glove
Raises Attack level by one.

Spike Spurs

Fly Bait

📍 Queensbury Crypt — Beat Midden in the Ancestral Chamber.
Glutton's Jug

Lightning Grip

Trinket Database FAQ

What are Trinkets in Mina the Hollower?

Trinkets are passive items that change Mina's combat, movement, survival, economy, Spark recovery, exploration, or cleanup routing.

Which Trinkets should I check first for 100% completion?

Start with easy-to-overlook reward, shop, boss, and hidden-route Trinkets. Then compare your save against the All Trinket Locations checklist and use the Checklist & Tracker Tool to mark progress.

Are Trinkets only useful for combat?

No. Some Trinkets improve damage, but others help with movement, survival, Bones farming, Spark recovery, exploration, or late-game cleanup.
