Pit Preserver

Nox's Bayou

Effect

Pit Preserver bounces Mina upward after falling into a pit or water and reduces the damage taken from the hazard.

Location

Nox's Bayou — purchased from the Kindly Hag merchant inside her house for 1,000 Bones.

Best Use

Pit Preserver is most useful in platforming-heavy areas, water-filled rooms, and routes where a missed jump would normally cost health or force a restart from the previous safe position.

Is Pit Preserver Worth Using?

It is a strong exploration Trinket when hazards are the main problem. For boss fights without pits or water, replace it with a Trinket that improves damage, healing, or defense.
