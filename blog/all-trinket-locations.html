Complete Guide

All Trinket Locations in Mina the Hollower

A complete checklist for finding Trinkets in Mina the Hollower, including area notes, effects, NPC rewards, shops, hidden routes, and Black Market recovery options.

How to use this guide

Trinkets are one of the most important progression systems in Mina the Hollower. Some improve combat, some help exploration, and some are tied to NPC rewards or hidden conditions.

Use this page as a completion checklist. For build recommendations, see our best trinkets guide.

Worried about missing a condition-based Trinket? Read the Missable Trinkets and Black Market recovery guide before doing final cleanup.

For a broader completion route, see the 100% cleanup checklist covering trinkets, weapons, Black Market recovery, mirrors, and achievements.

If you want to mark progress while playing, use the Mina the Hollower Checklist & Tracker Tool alongside this Trinket list.

If you are using this list for 100% cleanup, also check the mirror guide to plan backtracking and revisit routes more smoothly.

All Trinket Locations Checklist

# Trinket Effect Location Notes
1 Plasma Funnel Drink Plasma Vials twice as fast. Don't lose Vials when hit. Ossex Trinket Bazaar shop
2 Seismic Belt Press Attack while burrowing to emit a damaging pulse around you. Ossex Trinket Bazaar shop
3 Brisk Brew Walk faster. Gain a burst of speed for a short time after hitting a foe. Ossex Purchased from Lady Susan at the Trinket Bazaar.
4 Uranium Bracelet Deal and receive significantly more damage. High risk, high reward. Ossex Trinket Bazaar, 2000 Bones (also drops from Nether Kraken)
5 Intravenous Vial Automatically use a Plasma Vial when you have enough Plasma stored. Ossex Trinket Bazaar shop
6 Shock Flint Attack at full Plasma or full health to fire a deadly shock ball that grows over distance. Ossex Trinket Bazaar shop
7 Bubble Ring Strike foes quickly to form a protective shield that absorbs one hit. Ossex Purchased from Lady Susan after the Trinket Bazaar expands.
8 Counter Vial Dodge and launch a burst at the attacker if struck while readying a vial. Ossex Trinket Bazaar shop
9 Deboning Wand Enemies reward far more Bones, but the Bones bounce around erratically. Ossex Reward from Elizabeth at Ossex manor. Accept her offer, then repay 1,000 Bones.
10 Steady Soles Move normally on water, grass, stairs, and ice. Resist knockback, even into pits. Ossex Enter the house right of the fountain via the roof. Burrow under barricade, go through attic.
11 Valor Medallion Move slightly while healing. No Plasma lost when struck during a heal. Ossex Speak to Furl above the clothes store, hit his nose, bring him flowers, then burrow-boop him.
12 Bell of Grace Chance to keep Spark on death. On sparkless death, save half or all Bones. Ossex Attack Frayd to remove his Bones, then give him 1,000 Bones at the homeless camp.
13 Pneumatic Armlet Supercharge your throws. Thrown objects go faster, further, and deal more damage. Ossex Reward from Willis in the Ossex Music Hall.
14 Warding Beastium Take less damage, but healing is much less effective. Ossex Near Evra in the city.
15 Thermal Pack Boosts damage after death or exiting Underlab. Effect fades, then returns stronger. Ossex Return to the Miner's Den after saving Fiancé from Bone Beach.
16 Keri the Wisp Press Jump while airborne to float and extend your fall distance. Southern Outskirts Poppit the Merchant's secret cave near the trail to Bone Beach.
17 Primed Vial Pouch Carry 2 extra Plasma Vials. Using Vials always restores some health. Southern Outskirts Small house entered via the roof; chest across a few gaps inside.
18 Smelling Salts Raises Sidearm level by one. Eastern Heath Frozen Pass, accessed via the fast travel train system.
19 Willow the Wisp After taking damage, summon a friendly wisp that boosts attack damage and speed. Eastern Heath Poppit the Merchant's secret cave.
20 Windfall Charm Picking up Joules restores some Plasma. Foes hit by Sidearms can drop Joules. Eastern Heath Obtained from Lad, the kid flying the green kite.
21 Chain Capacitor Attack enemies without missing to boost damage. Missing resets the combo. Eastern Heath Destroy the Choppe Shoppe's vehicle within the time limit.
22 Twill Weave Raises Defense level by one. Nox's Bayou Cliff reached by rope near the big watery section at the start.
23 Vascular Syrup Health decreases very slowly after being hit. You cannot die until the effect runs out. Nox's Bayou Near Big Lagoon.
24 Pit Preserver Bounce up when falling into a pit or water. Pit damage is reduced. Nox's Bayou Buy from the Kindly Hag merchant in her house, 1,000 Bones.
25 Iron Lung Increases maximum burrow time, even when underwater. Nox's Bayou Reward for defeating the Mock Moon mini boss.
26 Tumbling Tutu Press Jump while airborne to spin attack and bounce off enemies. Nox's Bayou Return the small frog to the Bayou Band in Backwaters after beating Nox's Beast.
27 Volatile Beastium Defeated enemies sometimes drop a bomb. Nox's Bayou Buy from a merchant encountered on the Bone Beach–to–Bayou train route.
28 Helio the Wisp After taking damage, summon a friendly wisp that restores your health. Western Wilds Poppit the Merchant's secret cave.
29 Flame Guard Blocks one hit from fire or explosions, then breaks. Recharges after a few seconds. Western Wilds Inside the Molten Foundry.
30 Dummy Cache Press Heal to place a Joule-filled decoy that distracts enemies. Western Wilds Fulfill the dying old man's wish; requires visiting Coltrane Peak via train first.
31 Dead Leaf Hold still to turn into a silent statue. Breaks if you move. Western Wilds Reward from Leaf.
32 Lace Glove Raises Attack level by one. Western Wilds Beat Bugin twice with Ack and Dak. They can be found in multiple locations, including Western Wilds.
33 Spike Spurs Take less damage and walk steadfastly on spikes without being knocked back. Knight's Rest Behind a destructible wall leading to a large spike pit.
34 Desperation Bonnet Deal more damage at low health. Sidearms also cost less at low health. Mourner's Mile Locked tomb; return the statue's head to open it.
35 Proto Spark Saves Mina from death once with half health. Resets every time you die. Queensbury Crypt Complete the Duke questline by finding the Duchess.
36 Stolenoid Pulls nearby pickups toward Mina with magnetic force. Queensbury Crypt Buy from Belvedere's shop in Statue Head Hall, 600 Bones.
37 Fly Bait Attracts friendly flies when Mina defeats enemies. Queensbury Crypt Beat Midden in the Ancestral Chamber.
38 Glutton's Jug Equip this jug and defeat 50 enemies to fill it. Backwaters Received from Buffo. Quest stage of the Trinket that upgrades into Plasma Jug.
39 Plasma Jug Defeating enemies grants bonus Plasma. Plasma can fill above its normal maximum. Backwaters Defeat 50 enemies with Glutton's Jug equipped, return to Buffo, then defeat him. Same Trinket slot as Glutton's Jug.
40 Spark Catcher Grants one extra Spark Container. Hitting foes recovers lost Sparks; loose Sparks fly to you. Backwaters Buy from Pinky's Parlor, 500 Bones.
41 Marrow Flask +25% Bone pickup value. Dramatically speeds up Bone farming. Backwaters Standard chest in the eastern section.
42 Repulsing Root Hitting enemies pushes them back further. Great for crowd control. Septemburg Chest in hidden path west of the Carving Man arena.
43 Lightning Grip Attacks chain lightning between nearby enemies. Septemburg Crow Town area, yellow portal key required.
44 Spring Heels Jump after landing to perform a longer, higher jump. Radiant Manor Check during your Trinket cleanup route.
45 Niter Belt Creates a radial explosion when emerging from burrow. Sandfalls Check during your Trinket cleanup route.
46 Tunneling Codex Hold Jump to burrow repeatedly without needing to land. Sandfalls Score 5,000 points in the Ring Dive Parlor mini-game.
47 Joule Syringe Gain Plasma from Sidearms, unless at 1 HP. At 0 Joules, spend health to use Sidearms. Bone Beach Found in a treasure chest.
48 Bellows Bustle Press Jump while airborne to perform an air dash. Multiple Areas Complete all three races hosted by Blaise in Sandfalls, Ossex, and Western Wilds.
49 Dodging Pendulum Press Jump right before taking damage to dodge and become invulnerable. Radiant Manor Earned from a Mimic encounter.
48A Thorn Harness Poison damage reduced by 60%. Essential before entering Nox's Bayou. Bone Beach NPC reward in the southeastern coastal area.
49 Shadowhide Taking damage while burrowed does not interrupt the burrow state. Bone Beach Reward from secret boss in the eastern cave.
50 Starving Beastium Attacks slowly drain enemy max health over time. Coltrane Peak Chest in the upper mountainside section.
51 Draining Beastium Attacks steal a small amount of health from enemies. Coltrane Peak Buy from the peak merchant, 800 Bones.
51A Reckless Beastium After healing, your next few hits are powered up, but you're more vulnerable. Mirror's End Reward from Dr. Naught.
52 Burning Beastium Create damaging fireballs when hit. Jump or burrow to avoid them. Coltrane Peak Found in Coltrane Peak.
53 Watchful Eye Earn extra Bones for defeating a foe unharmed, as judged worthy by the eye. Coltrane Peak Purchased from Rupert.
54 Blinking Glass Sometimes, avoid taking damage, and become briefly invulnerable. Coltrane Peak Check Coltrane Peak during Trinket cleanup.
55 Polyp Lamp Illuminates dark areas and stuns enemies caught in the light. Radiant Manor Standard chest in the basement.
56 Wallower's Gauntlets Allows Mina to burrow into walls. Kindlewood Found in a chest.
57 Oozing Organ Leave a damaging trail of ooze behind you whenever you burrow. Kindlewood Purchase it from the vendor inside the Madd House, just outside the farm area.
58 Voltaic Guard Taking damage sends out an electric burst that damages nearby enemies. Mirror's End Chest past the third mirror gate.
58A Bridge Weaver Step off a ledge to summon a web-weaving spider and cross gaps. Astral Orrery Found in a chest.
59 Vial Salvo Unused Plasma Vials convert to projectiles when your Vial stock is full. Astral Orrery Reward from defeating the Gate Warden mini boss.
60 Evasion Powder Extends post-hit invulnerability window. Pairs perfectly with Vascular Syrup. Astral Orrery Final chest before the endgame boss corridor.
