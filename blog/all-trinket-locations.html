All Trinket Locations in Mina the Hollower

A complete checklist for finding Trinkets in Mina the Hollower, including area notes, effects, NPC rewards, shops, hidden routes, and Black Market recovery options.

Quick Answer: How Many Trinkets Are There?

Mina the Hollower has 60 collectible Trinket slots. Glutton's Jug upgrades into Plasma Jug, so this guide lists both forms separately under the same collectible number.

Use the table below to compare each Trinket's effect, location, and acquisition method. Trinket names with dedicated database pages are clickable for more detailed guides.

How to use this guide

Trinkets are one of the most important progression systems in Mina the Hollower. Some improve combat, some help exploration, and some are tied to NPC rewards or hidden conditions.

Use this page as a completion checklist. For build recommendations, see our best trinkets guide.

Worried about missing a condition-based Trinket? Read the Missable Trinkets and Black Market recovery guide before doing final cleanup.
