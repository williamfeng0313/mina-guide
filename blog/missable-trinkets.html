Missable Trinkets in Mina the Hollower

A focused guide to easy-to-overlook Trinkets, NPC reward conditions, hidden pickups, and recovery checks for players cleaning up all 60 Trinkets.

Quick Answer: Are any Trinkets permanently missable?

Based on the current checklist, no Trinket should be treated as permanently lost without first checking its original unlock condition, revisiting the relevant area or NPC, and confirming whether a documented recovery option exists.

Most Trinkets are better treated as easy to overlook rather than permanently missable. Before assuming a Trinket is lost, check its original area, NPC reward, shop, hidden route, or any later recovery option documented for that specific item.

In the current 60-Trinket checklist, Dead Leaf is one of the easiest Trinkets to double-check during Western Wilds cleanup. Several other Trinkets are also easy to overlook because they depend on NPCs, optional quests, hidden rooms, shops, or specific rewards.

If you are cleaning up everything for completion, use the Mina the Hollower 100% cleanup checklist after checking missed trinkets.

Some missed trinkets are easier to clean up once you understand revisits and mirror routes. See the mirror guide before doing your final 100% sweep.

Check Recovery Options Before Starting Over

Some Trinkets tied to conditions, NPCs, or missed interactions may have alternative recovery options, but recovery is not identical for every Trinket.

Before assuming an item is permanently lost, first confirm its normal acquisition method, revisit the relevant NPC or area, and then check whether a documented Black Market or alternative recovery path exists.

For item-specific recovery notes, see the Black Market Trinkets guide.

Completion Cleanup Order

  1. Open the All Trinket Locations checklist.
  2. Use Ctrl+F to search the Trinket name you are missing.
  3. Check whether it is a shop item, NPC reward, hidden room, boss reward, or quest condition.
  4. For NPC rewards, revisit the NPC after major story events.
  5. For condition-based or missed rewards, check whether a documented recovery option exists.
  6. Only then assume you need to backtrack through the original area.

FAQ

Is Dead Leaf missable?

Dead Leaf is an easy Trinket to double-check during Western Wilds cleanup. Its current location note should be treated as Dead Leaf — Western Wilds — Reward from Leaf.

Can missed Trinkets be recovered?

Some missed or condition-based Trinkets may have recovery options, but you should first check the normal location, NPC reward, and related Black Market notes before assuming a Trinket is permanently lost.

Which Trinkets should completion players double-check?

Start with the full Trinket list, then review NPC or quest reward Trinkets such as Dead Leaf, Deboning Wand, Valor Medallion, Bell of Grace, Thermal Pack, Tumbling Tutu, Proto Spark, and Wallower's Gauntlets.
