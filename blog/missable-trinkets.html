Completion Safety Guide

Missable Trinkets in Mina the Hollower

A focused guide to easy-to-overlook Trinkets, NPC reward conditions, and Black Market recovery notes for players cleaning up all 60 Trinkets.

Quick Answer: Are any Trinkets permanently missable?

Based on the current checklist, no Trinket should be treated as permanently lost without first checking the Black Market and the original unlock condition.

The key warning is this: Trinkets marked with ⚠ are technically missable, but may be purchased retroactively from the Black Market if the required condition has been met.

In the current 60-Trinket checklist, Dead Leaf is the clearest explicitly marked missable Trinket. Several other Trinkets are not marked as missable, but are easy to overlook because they depend on NPCs, optional quests, hidden rooms, or specific rewards.

Explicitly Marked Missable Trinket

Trinket Effect Original Condition Recovery Note
Dead Leaf ⚠ Hold still to turn into a silent statue. Breaks if you move. Crush the leaf NPC in Kindelwood, then attend its funeral in the Western Wilds. Marked as technically missable. Check the Black Market if the condition was completed later.

Easy-to-Miss NPC and Quest Trinkets

These Trinkets are not necessarily permanently missable, but they are easy to overlook because they depend on talking to the right NPC, completing a small condition, or returning after another event.

How Black Market Recovery Works

The current checklist notes that Trinkets marked with ⚠ are technically missable but can be purchased later from the Black Market if the required condition is met after the fact.

That means you should not panic immediately if a condition-based Trinket looks lost. First, confirm whether you triggered the original condition. Then check the Black Market before assuming the Trinket is gone.

Need more detail? Read the Black Market Trinkets recovery guide for cleanup order, recovery checks, and condition-based Trinket notes.

Completion Cleanup Order

  1. Open the All Trinket Locations checklist.
  2. Use Ctrl+F to search the Trinket name you are missing.
  3. Check whether it is a shop item, NPC reward, hidden room, boss reward, or quest condition.
  4. For NPC rewards, revisit the NPC after major story events.
  5. For ⚠ Trinkets, check the Black Market after meeting the condition.
  6. Only then assume you need to backtrack through the original area.

FAQ

Is Dead Leaf missable?

Dead Leaf is marked with ⚠ in the current checklist. Its original condition involves crushing the leaf NPC in Kindelwood and then attending its funeral in the Western Wilds.

Can missed Trinkets be recovered?

The current database note says ⚠ Trinkets can be purchased retroactively from the Black Market if the condition is met after the fact. Always check the Black Market before assuming a Trinket is permanently lost.

Which Trinkets should completion players double-check?

Start with Dead Leaf, then review NPC or quest reward Trinkets such as Deboning Wand, Valor Medallion, Bell of Grace, Thermal Pack, Tumbling Tutu, Proto Spark, and Wallower's Gauntlets.
