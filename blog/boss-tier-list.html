Boss Tier List (First Clear)

Mina the Hollower Boss Tier List

Quick Answer: Hardest Bosses in Mina the Hollower

This Mina the Hollower boss tier list ranks bosses by first-clear difficulty, pattern pressure, arena control, preparation needs, and how punishing each fight feels before you fully understand the mechanics.

For most players, the hardest fights are not only about damage. They are hard because they combine visual clutter, movement pressure, hazards, adds, mirror routing, or long cleanup routes. This list is based on a standard first playthrough, not speedruns, NG+, or challenge restrictions.

S Tier — Brutal

Astral Gate Warden — A late-game wall that tests routing, mirrors, survival, and loadout discipline. This kind of fight is brutal because the pressure comes from the full route around it, not just the boss arena itself.

A Tier — Hard

Nether Kraken, The Worm's Back, Worm Queen, Dark Deluxy — These fights can feel difficult because of pattern density, movement pressure, secret requirements, or awkward punish windows. They are learnable, but they punish impatient play hard.

B Tier — Normal

Carving Man, Major Miner, Tide Caller — These bosses have clearer patterns and more forgiving windows. They can still kill you, but they usually become manageable once you slow down and learn when to attack.

How This Boss Tier List Is Ranked

  • Pattern pressure: how hard the boss is to read on a first clear.
  • Arena danger: hazards, walls, adds, and movement restrictions.
  • Loadout pressure: whether the fight feels much harder without a good build.
  • Recovery difficulty: how punishing mistakes feel during the fight.
  • Cleanup relevance: whether the boss matters for 100% completion or late-game checks.

Recommended Before Hard Bosses

If a boss feels unfair, check your weapon, Trinkets, and cleanup progress before forcing attempts. Mina rewards preparation more than rushing.

Boss Tier List FAQ

What is the hardest boss in Mina the Hollower?

The hardest boss depends on your build and route, but late-game fights with heavy pressure, hazards, mirror routing, or long gauntlets tend to feel the most punishing on a first clear.

Should I change builds for hard bosses?

Yes. If you are dying before learning patterns, use a safer build. If you already understand the fight, switch toward more damage. The best boss build is the one that solves your current problem.

Is this tier list for 100% completion?

This list is mainly for first-clear difficulty, but some boss and secret fights also matter during 100% cleanup. Use the 100% Cleanup Checklist if you are tracking completion.
