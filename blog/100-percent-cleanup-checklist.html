Step 2: Complete the Trinket Collection

Trinkets are one of the largest cleanup categories. Current community databases document 61 Trinkets, including normal pickups, NPC rewards, shops, quest rewards, upgraded forms, and recovery routes.

Use these Trinket guides

  • All Trinket Locations — compare your collection against the full list.
  • Missable and Easy-to-Miss Trinkets — review rewards and routes commonly overlooked.
  • Black Market Trinkets — check whether a missed item can be recovered.

Recommended Trinket cleanup order

  1. Compare your inventory with the full list.
  2. Mark every item you already own in the tracker.
  3. Review quest and NPC rewards.
  4. Revisit areas opened by later movement tools.
  5. Check recovery options only after checking normal acquisition routes.

Step 3: Defeat Optional and Secret Bosses

Finishing the story does not mean every boss has been defeated. Several encounters are hidden behind side quests, shops, dialogue choices, special doors, optional routes, and late-game activities.

  • Check the prologue Nether Kraken encounter.
  • Review all regional and Spark Generator bosses.
  • Complete side-quest boss encounters.
  • Check secret bosses unlocked through NPC and shop interactions.
  • Confirm all post-fight rewards were collected.

Use the Mina the Hollower Secret Bosses Guide for locations, unlock conditions, and rewards.

Step 4: Complete Weapons and Sidearms

Completion tracking should include both Mina's main weapons and the Sidearms collected throughout Tenebrous Isle. Check acquisition, upgrades, dual-Sidearm access, and any achievement tied to a specific weapon or Sidearm.

Equipment checklist

  • All five main weapons unlocked
  • Weapon upgrades completed
  • All available Sidearms collected
  • Dual-Sidearm access checked
  • Sidearm-specific achievements reviewed
  • Trinket and weapon combinations tested where required

Read the Best Weapons Guide for loadout recommendations.

Step 5: Finish Side Quests and Minigames

Side quests can unlock Trinkets, bosses, equipment, minigames, NPC progression, and achievement conditions. A completed story route may still leave several quest chains unfinished.

  • Revisit NPCs after major regional milestones.
  • Check whether quest rewards were collected.
  • Complete fishing and other recurring activities.
  • Review racing, Ring Dive, Wrecker, and similar challenges.
  • Finish quest chains that unlock optional bosses.

Step 6: Review All 50 Achievements

Mina the Hollower has 50 achievements or Feats covering story progression, challenge runs, Sidearm tasks, Trinket objectives, collection goals, and minigames.

Some achievements unlock naturally during the main story, while others require specific equipment, unusual combat setups, strict challenge conditions, or activities that are easy to miss during a normal playthrough.

Use the Achievement Difficulty and Cleanup Guide before attempting challenge-based achievements or starting another playthrough.

Achievement categories

  • 8 story achievements: tied to major progression milestones and story encounters.
  • 12 challenge achievements: special runs and restricted objectives that may require careful planning.
  • 15 Sidearm achievements: combat tasks requiring specific Sidearms, enemy setups, timing, or special interactions.
  • 7 Trinket achievements: objectives connected to Trinket effects, equipment combinations, and hidden mechanics.
  • 5 collection achievements: rewards for completing major collectible or equipment categories.
  • 3 minigame achievements: objectives connected to optional activities and side challenges.

Achievement cleanup checklist

  • ✓ Complete all story achievements
  • ✓ Review challenge-run restrictions before starting
  • ✓ Complete all Sidearm-specific achievements
  • ✓ Complete all Trinket-specific achievements
  • ✓ Finish collection achievements
  • ✓ Complete all minigame achievements
  • ✓ Check setup-based achievements separately
  • ✓ Record achievements that require another playthrough

Important challenge-run warning

Achievements such as Below Zero may have strict rules involving upgrades, pickups, equipment, or other actions. Confirm the exact restrictions before beginning the run, because some mistakes may require restarting the attempt.

Setup-based achievements such as Bounding Bonanza should also be tracked separately instead of assuming they will unlock naturally during story progression.

Step 7: Check Upgrades and Collectibles

A final equipment sweep should include permanent upgrades, capacity increases, regional collectibles, and travel infrastructure.

  • Health upgrades
  • Plasma Vial capacity
  • Spark Containers
  • Joule capacity upgrades
  • Trinket Bags and equipment slots
  • Bonestones and other regional collectibles
  • Mirrors, train stations, Underlabs, and shortcuts
  • Fish trophies and fishing rewards
  • Songscrolls or equivalent music collectibles

Step 8: Review Endings and Missable Content

Before ending the run, review content that may depend on early-game decisions, dialogue, quest timing, or the prologue.

  • Optional prologue encounters
  • Normal ending requirements
  • Hidden ending requirements
  • Dialogue-triggered encounters
  • Quest rewards that may require another NPC visit
  • Challenge conditions that cannot be repaired mid-run

Read the Hidden Secrets Guide before considering the save complete.

Step 9: Prepare Before Starting New Game+

Do not enter New Game+ until you have checked every item or objective that may be easier to finish on the current save.

  • Mark completed categories in the interactive tracker.
  • Spend or bank resources that do not carry over normally.
  • Confirm missed prologue or challenge content.
  • Record remaining achievements requiring another run.
  • Review your preferred weapon and equipment setup.

Final recommendation

Keep the current save available until you have confirmed that the remaining goals genuinely require another playthrough.

FAQ

Is 100% completion hard in Mina the Hollower?

It is doable, but cleanup can become messy if you do not track missed trinkets, revisit paths, weapons, mirrors, and achievement-specific tasks.

What should I check first for 100%?

Start with missed trinkets, then check Black Market recovery, then compare against the full all-trinket list.

Are trinkets the hardest part of cleanup?

For many players, yes. Trinkets are easy to overlook because some are hidden, conditional, or tied to cleanup routes instead of the main path.

Do weapons matter for 100%?

Yes. A safer weapon can make backtracking and cleanup less frustrating, especially when revisiting older areas with awkward enemies or hazards.

Should I use a checklist?

Yes. Mina rewards exploration, but 100% completion is much easier when you track what you already checked instead of relying only on memory.

How many achievements are in Mina the Hollower?

Mina the Hollower has 50 achievements or trophies, covering story progress, challenge runs, Sidearms, Trinkets, collections, and minigames.

How many Trinkets are in Mina the Hollower?

Current completion databases document 61 Trinkets, including upgraded and special forms.

Do secret bosses count toward a complete cleanup?

They should be included in a full completion checklist, even when they are not required to reach the normal ending.

Should I finish everything before New Game+?

Finish any content that is easier to complete on the current save, then use New Game+ for missed encounters, challenge runs, and achievements that require another playthrough.

Does the interactive checklist save progress?

Yes. The checklist stores progress locally in the browser, so you can leave the page and continue later on the same browser.
