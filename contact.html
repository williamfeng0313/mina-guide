Corrections and Guide Feedback

Mina the Hollower is a large game with hidden items, optional interactions, and evolving community discoveries.

If you notice information that appears incorrect or outdated, please include as much detail as possible, such as:

  • The page URL.
  • The name of the Trinket, boss, area, or guide.
  • What information appears to be incorrect.
  • The correct information or source, when available.

Detailed reports help us review and correct information faster.
