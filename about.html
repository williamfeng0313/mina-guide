About MinaHollower A battered field journal for Mina the Hollower players.

MinaHollower is a fan-made guide site for players exploring Tenebrous Isle. The goal is simple: document the game clearly, organize the discoveries, and help players find useful answers without digging through scattered posts.

What this site is

MinaHollower is an unofficial, fan-made guide site for Mina the Hollower by Yacht Club Games. It is built as a central reference for players who want boss strategies, Trinket locations, build ideas, tools, and completion help in one place.

The style of the site follows the tone of the game: gothic, old-world, and slightly worn. Instead of a generic dark game portal, MinaHollower is designed like a battered adventure journal: deep forest green, bone-white text, candle-orange accents, and parchment-style panels.

What we cover

The guide is organized around four major sections:

  • Blog: beginner guides, boss advice, build explanations, mistakes, and hidden mechanics.
  • Database: Trinkets, bosses, weapons, and reference-style archive pages.
  • Tools: build planner, build quiz, checklist, and progress tracking helpers.
  • Completion support: pages designed for players chasing full collection and 100% completion.

Why the site exists

Players often find answers scattered across forum posts, videos, comments, and short wiki entries. MinaHollower exists to turn those scattered questions into organized pages: what to use, where to go, what to avoid, and what matters next.

Update philosophy

The site is intended to grow as more routes, boss strategies, Trinket combinations, and hidden interactions are documented. Pages may be revised as better information becomes available. When something is uncertain, it should be treated as a working note, not a final truth.
