Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Tenebrous Isle Guide collects, uses, and protects information when you visit minahollower.com.

1. Who We Are

Tenebrous Isle Guide is an independent, fan-made guide website for Mina the Hollower.

Our website address is: https://www.minahollower.com

This website is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Yacht Club Games.

2. Information We Collect

We may automatically collect limited technical information when you visit the website, such as browser type, device type, referring pages, approximate geographic information, pages viewed, and general usage information.

We do not currently require users to create accounts or directly provide personal information in order to use our guides, databases, or tools.

Some tools may store progress or preferences locally in your browser using local storage. This information remains on your device unless otherwise stated.

3. Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand how visitors use the website, which pages are popular, and how the site can be improved.

The website currently uses Vercel Analytics. Analytics services may collect technical and usage information such as page visits, device information, referral sources, and general performance data.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website and third-party services used by this website may use cookies, local storage, web beacons, or similar technologies.

These technologies may be used to remember preferences, measure website traffic, understand site usage, improve performance, prevent abuse, and support advertising.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features or third-party services.

5. Advertising and Google AdSense

We may use Google AdSense or other third-party advertising services to display advertisements.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's previous visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies may enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this website and other websites on the Internet.

Third-party advertising providers may also use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to measure advertising effectiveness and personalize advertising where permitted.

Users can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services by reviewing Google's privacy and advertising information.

6. Consent and Privacy Choices

Where required by applicable law, visitors may be asked for consent before certain cookies, advertising technologies, or personalized advertising features are used.

We may use a consent management platform or privacy message to allow visitors to manage their privacy and advertising choices.

7. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, advertising, site performance, or other website functionality.

These third parties may process information according to their own privacy policies. We do not control the privacy practices of third-party services.

8. External Links

Tenebrous Isle Guide may contain links to external websites, including game-related websites, community platforms, social networks, and other third-party resources.

We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of external websites.

9. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed at children under the age required for independent consent in their jurisdiction.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

10. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, subject to the practices of third-party service providers.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal information, including rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing.

Because this website currently collects very limited information directly from users, many requests may relate to data processed by third-party services.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when our website, services, analytics tools, advertising partners, or legal obligations change.

The latest version will always be published on this page.
